Who Is The Consultation For?

Free medical check-up and weight loss consultation for Chicago residents who are looking to lose weight so they can live a long, vibrant and healthy life while looking good and breaking the scale.

How Does It Work?

In this exam, our certified nurse or physician will do various tests to check your overall health level and help choose a doctor-supervised weight loss program that works best for you.

We Can Help You

For those of us who struggle with our weight it’s easy to lose hope. We have seen so many tricks, gimmicks and weight loss programs that simply do not work for us. They want us to eat like a pigeon, avoid all the things we love to eat and work out 'til we cry. We are here to tell you that it doesn’t' have to be that way. There is hope! Technology has advanced rapidly in recent years and now, with proper guidance, a doctor-supervised weight loss program, and a powerful community, you can lose weight and get back to living the full life you love!

You can join the ranks of our over 15,000 satisfied clients who have lost weight, regained their confidence and are living happy exuberant lives with their family. They did this without: breaking the bank, sweating buckets in intense workout programs, and most importantly, without giving up ice-cream, bread, pizza, doughnuts and all their favorite foods.
